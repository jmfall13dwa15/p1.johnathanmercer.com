My Mission


I have been lucky enough to win the cosmological and evolutionary lottery. I'm a caucasian male from a relatively affluent family in the United States in the 21th century. This situation has afforded me the opportunity to reach a high level of consciousness. The rub is, once you accidently take the red pill, you become painfully aware of the fact that there is no evidence (mass cultural delusions aside) of a loving god or an afterlife. Therefore it seems the only reasonable thing left to do, however in vain it may be, is to spend my life investigating the laws that created us in an attempt to figure out why are we used as survival machines by these genes, forced to love, only to have everything we care about ripped away from us in this near hopeless game of life.
